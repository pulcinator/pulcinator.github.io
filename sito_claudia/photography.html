estintoproject.

estinto is a photographic project about the deep connection between humans and some of the animals in danger of extinction. the Tlingit art style of the paintings highlights this connection and it is meant to make us understand the importance of this environmental problem and what are we doing to our earth.

there’s only one imperative: we must rediscover the connection with the nature.
